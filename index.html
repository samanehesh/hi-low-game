Game Instruction

The "Hi-Low World" game is a one or two player game with three difficulty range (Easy 1-1024, Medium 1-2048, Hard 1-4096). The objective of this game is to guess a secret number in as few guesses as possible. The secret number is generated within the predefined range by computer in one player mode or entered by the other player in two player mode.
